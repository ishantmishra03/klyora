Usage Tips:
( Dark on Light UI ): Use Midnight Blue for text/icons on a white background 

CTA Buttons: Midnight Blue button with Lavender Tint hover effect.

Card UI: White cards on Cool Gray sections with soft Royal Indigo borders.

Typography: Stick to dark blue (#0A1F44) for primary titles and muted gray for supporting text.
